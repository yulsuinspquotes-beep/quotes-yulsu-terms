Community Guidelines

Yulsu is a global community of learners

We believe that everyone should have access to good education. Our guidelines are meant to build a mutual understanding of what being a part of this community is all about. We will take action if any of these guidelines are not upheld, so please read carefully.

Always be Respectful

We come together from across the world with the same goal in mind - to learn. Curiosity, questioning, and cultural understanding are something we celebrate. Be respectful of others and where they're coming from.

Help and support across all ideas

We are all in this together. Life is hard and takes a lot of courage and dedication to grow. If someone shares you a quote and you think it's not good to you, don't reply badly. Can't say it nicely? Don't weigh in.

Think before you share

We care about your safety. The intention is to share good quotes! But don't over-share. Sharing and encouraging others is great but be careful.

Please don't use Yulsu to...

  • Upload or input bad quotes: Yulsu is a safe place for learners of all backgrounds. Harassment and hurtful content will not be tolerated.
  • Script or cheat maliciously: Yulsu believes in honest learning.
  • Write inflammatory comments: Hateful, obscene and off-topic comments don't contribute to be inspired.

To Summarize

We do not tolerate content that is: Illegal, Pornographic, Excessively profane or violent, Spam, Threatening, harassing, or bullying, Associated with racism or intolerance, Impersonating someone in a misleading or deceptive manner, Personal confidential information.

By following these guidelines, we will all contribute to an interesting and helpful learning community.
